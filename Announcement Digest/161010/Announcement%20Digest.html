Det 415 Cadet Assembled Test Bank (CATB)

Academic Support Flight is instituting a new program in the Cadet wing. The Cadet Assembled Test Bank (CATB) will be a bank of previously taken tests for a variety of classes. It will consist of a physical binder in the armory, as well as a USB stick with digital versions of the tests. Cadets are asked to bring in any materials from classes they have previously taken and are willing to submit to the CATB and deliver them to Academic Support Flight. More details can be found in the memorandum HERE. Please contact C/Nguyen with any questions.
