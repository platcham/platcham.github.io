NEED TO KNOW

Male Locker Room

Attention Male Cadets: the locker room is in a state of disarray. There are uniform items out of lockers, backpacks and civilian clothes lying around, and trash on the floor. This is not professional. The policy on locker rooms is all items need to be contained within your locker  with the exception of shoes/boots, which may be kept either on top or underneath your locker. Backpacks and other items may not be stored in the locker rooms. Backpacks are to be stored in the cadet hangar or in your cubby, where you have plenty of space.

I understand that many cadets only have half a locker. That being said, we're taking feedback on what can be improved in our locker rooms! The survey is at THIS LINK.
