AAS Food Drive

The Arnold Society Pledge Flight (KAC Flight) is hosting a Food Drive and will be collecting canned goods all week. We will be going up against the other branches to see who can collect the most, with a special prize for the winning branch.

Donations can be put in boxes on the first floor north side, and/or donated during night events that will be held in the Armory gym throughout the week. If there are any questions please contact either C/ Barajas or C/ Maddox.
