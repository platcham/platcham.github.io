Current Events

Airman Missing at Guam Presumed Dead


The Airman who went missing after going for a swim at Anderson Air Force Base is now presumed dead. The Hawaii Air National Guard has led search-and-recovery efforts, but have not found any sign of the missing Airman.

Air Force Testing New Maternity Uniforms


The Air Force is testing new maternity Uniforms. Sixty female Airmen tested a prototype, with hopes of them being available next October. It takes around three years to get a new uniform out and approved. The uniform stretches more, has more pockets, and looks more like the normal uniform.

New Combat Medicine Technologies


Researchers are working on a quicker way to administer blood to wounded troops. Powdered blood is being looked at as well as spray on skin for burn victims. These methods are being developed so that a surgeon can operate without the tools available in an operating room.
