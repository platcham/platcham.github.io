Current Events

Osprey Flights in Japan Halted After Emergency Landing


The commanding general of the III Marine Expeditionary Force has halted all MV-22 flights in Japan due after one of the aircraft was forced to make an emergency landing off the coast of Okinawa. The aircraft had been refueling when one of the rotors hit the refueling line, damaging the aircraft. Two of the five crewman are still in the hospital. The Osprey will remain grounded until the safety procedures can be reviewed.

Marine Corps Receives First Amphibious Combat Vehicles from BAE


Four out of the 16 ordered amphibious vehicles have been delivered to the Marine Corps. The new vehicle was commissioned to replace the amphibious assault vehicles. The new vehicle runs on 8 wheels instead of tracks, and is supposed to be much faster in water and on land.

Trump Picks Elon Musk and Travis Kalanick to Join Policy Forum


President Elect Trump has picked Tesla Founder Elon Musk and Uber CEO Travis Kalanick to join the Strategic and Policy Forum. Also on the Forum is PepsiCo Chairman Indra Nooyi. This forum will meet with Trump regularly to help advise him on business and economic plans while he is President.
