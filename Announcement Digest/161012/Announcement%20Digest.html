"M"azing Race Volunteers


C/Braun is looking for is volunteers who would want to help out with seting up and running the ROTC portion of the "M"azing race event. He needs people on 19 October from 1630-2030 working shifts. The way we have it now is two blocks of time with one cadet at each block (other services will be providing people too). He is also looking for extremely motivated cadets to actually compete in the race (and to have a friendly competition against the other services.)  If you are interested, contact C/Braun.

Form 50 Clarification

Besides filling out a Form 50 for an illness, you must also submit a Doctor's note to Captain Johnson.  Unless you have otherwise coordinated with Captain Johnson, not submitting a doctor's note will result in an unexcused absence, which again counts against the 80% attendance requirement.

Keep In Mind...

Det 415 Cadet Assembled Test Bank (CATB)

Academic Support Flight is instituting a new program in the Cadet wing. The Cadet Assembled Test Bank (CATB) will be a bank of previously taken tests for a variety of classes. It will consist of a physical binder in the armory, as well as a USB stick with digital versions of the tests. Cadets are asked to bring in any materials from classes they have previously taken and are willing to submit to the CATB and deliver them to Academic Support Flight. More details can be found in the memorandum HERE. Please contact C/Nguyen with any questions.
