NEED TO KNOW

Deep Clean and Locker Rooms


Cleaning Group B has cleaning this week.

This week Facilities is conducting a deep clean of the armory over 3 days from Wednesday-Friday. There are cleaning tasks posted on the hangar board, in addition to the weekly cleaning chart. All cadets, regardless of cleaning group, are to help participate in the deep clean. This is to ensure our facility is kept to the high standard we expect of ourselves!

Regarding locker rooms - cadets are not permitted to have personal belongings (other than shoes) left unsecured outside of their lockers. If you choose to have shoes or boots outside of your locker, please organize them in a neat manner. All cadets are to have a lock on their locker and cadets found in violation of this will be counseled. AS100s and 250s have until COB Wednesday to have a lock on their locker.

Please contact Cadet Pagnano with any questions or concerns.
