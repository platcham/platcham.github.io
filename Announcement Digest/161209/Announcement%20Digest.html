Current Events

Astronaut John Glenn Dead at 95


The all-american astronaut John Glen died yesterday at the age of 95. He was a Marine Corps pilot that flew 149 missions in WWII and Korea, was the third man in space, and the first man to orbit the earth. He was also the oldest man to enter space in Discovery in 1998.

US Bombs ISIS Hospital


The US bombed a hospital in Mosul, under the request of Iraqi forces, under the attack of Islamic State fighters. The attack only took place because of the Iraqi request and the confirmation of ISIS fighters firing from the building.

50,000 ISIS Fighters Killed


A military official has reported that over 50,000 ISIS members have been killed since the start of the war on terror. A meeting took place to discuss the future use of special operations to counter extremist threats.
