PT Uniform Wear


For clarification on wearing the Air Force PT gear, in accordance with AFI 36-2903 under 7.1.12:
"PTU/IPTU items are authorized for wear with conservative civilian/personal attire during individual/personal PT or while off-duty (e.g. PT shirt with personal shorts/pants, PT jacket with personal shirt/pants/shorts, etc.). No civilian/personal items with offensive wording, graphics or photos are to be worn with the PTU/IPTU items at any time."

Current Events

Rifle Change Considered by Marine Corps


The switch from the M16 to the M4 was widely praised, however the M27 is being considered by the Marine Corps. The rife has a longer effective range, is said to be more accurate, and has a full-auto mode. This is being tested and debated for cost and overall effectiveness.

Video of U.S. Soldiers Being Shot in Jordan Surfaces


Security footage captured the killing of three US soldiers in Jordan, early November. The investigation is not complete, but it is being looked at as a deliberate attack on those specific Americans. Special Operations Command is performing a separate investigation. The three Americans killed were members of the 5th Special Forces group at Fort Campbell in Kentucky

Pilot Shortage May Lead To Pay Increase


The US military is concerned with the loss of pilots to the private sector, leaving the Air Force short several hundred pilots. Pay is looked at as the issue, as the pay tends to be better outside of the Defense Department. There is a potential increase in Aviation Retention Pay, in hopes of increasing the numbers.
