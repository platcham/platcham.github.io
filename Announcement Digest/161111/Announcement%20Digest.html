Fall 2016 Dining Out


Events Flight has been hard at work, and they are happy to announce officially this semester's dining out. The Fall 2016 Dining Out will be held on Saturday, 3 December at the Embassy Suites in Bloomington. Woolley's Steakhouse will be catering a delicious meal as we celebrate the end of the semester.

If you don't already know this years dining event we are allowed to bring guests, which gives both you, as ROTC cadets, and the guests a unique perspective on an Air Force tradition. Here is an online invitation to fill out. Please RSVP by next Friday, 18 November. The cost will be $33 to attend.

More details will be given at LLAB. Contact C/Braun with any questions.

Volunteers needed for A Night at the Armory.


As I am sure you are aware by now, this coming Friday 18 November is our Annual Night at the Armory from 18:00-21:00.

This event allows us to introduce potential cadets to why life in AFROTC is awesome, and a big part of the reason why it is so, is you.

As such, we need volunteers to help out doing fun events such as leading GLP's running a lemonade stand and helping Capt Johnson with the virtual rifle range.

Please sign up using this link and enter your details ASAP. If you are a member of Eagles or Arnies or the running team etc please put that in the final column. This is always a great night, and could be a really easy way for a particular flight to run away with a lot of honor points.

There once again may be free food available....there usually is.

Please contact C/Bryson with any questions.

GMC Parkas!


Attention all GMC, the Parkas are being made available to you! All 100s interested in being issued a parka will need to watch for the sign up link in Monday the 14th's digest. NOTICE: the time to sign up will be between 1800 and midnight. This is a hard deadline. We can see what time you sign up so remember, integrity first.

200s, you will have the ability to sign up on Tuesday the 15th during the same time window. Remember, there is a limited number of parkas available and all will be on a first come first serve basis. That is why it is important to sign up if you truly want one. To all other cadets and POC, any remaining will be made available to you based on what remains.
