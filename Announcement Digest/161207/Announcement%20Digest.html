Current Events

The Air Force's Antarctic Mission


Every February to October, roughly 100 Airmen stay at McMurdo station to support the Polar Program Research. The mission is to get supplies to the South Pole station, in Operation Deep Freeze in Antarctica. This is a huge and very cold Airlift operation requiring Airmen to put in many hours in this cold.

Retired Military Excluded From New Concealed Carry Policy


The new Firearm carry law is not intended for retired military, but rather service members on DoD property. It is still up to the discretion of the Commander, O-5 and above, and you must be over the age of 21.

No Women Currently in SpecOps Training


There are no women currently in training for special operations, according to the US Air Force. Interest by women in special operations is increasing, however. There was a female in TACP training that has become injured, Secretary of Defense Carter did not want anyone to be confused. There is still debate on women in combat roles, but recruiting is increasing for women.
