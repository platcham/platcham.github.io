MWR Elite 6 Ops Availability


If you don’t know what Elite6Ops is, google them and prepare to play the coolest game of laser tag known to man.  Using replica gas powered M4s/M16s with recoil, you battle through a village consisting of multiple level buildings against the opposing team.  This is hands down the coolest MWR event I have done being in the program.  Cost is still being finalized with company, though it will be around $25/person.  Ask anyone who did it last year and they will tell you how much fun it was.  CLICK HERE for a sign-up sheet for what days will work best.  Fill this out as soon as you can.  If money is an issue, or if you have any questions, please contact Cadet Amundson at 320.493.5871

Intramurals are in full swing, come support!

Come support the soccer crew Monday nights!  Intramural games are played under the lights at TCF Bank Stadium and fans can hang out down on the field.  We meet at the Armory half an hour before the game starts and transition over together. This week’s game is at 10:10PM (3 Oct).  We will be leaving the armory at 9:40PM. Contact Cadet Amundson (320.493.5871) with questions.
