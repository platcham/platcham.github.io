Printer Paper - Please be Conservative!

Just a reminder that printer paper is paid for by the Flying Gophers Student Group. Please be considerate when printing at the Armory by trying to conserve paper. This means NO printing lab manuals or other excessive documents. Remember that CSE students also have access to $60 worth of printing in CSE labs.

AS100s Study Abroad Opportunity

100s,

What are you planning on doing during spring break? Is it as cool as going to London and learning about World War II from the British perspective? As freshmen, you have the unique opportunity to participate in Freshmen Seminars Abroad. How these work is you register for the spring semester course (which fulfills Global Perspectives and Writing Intensive lib ed requirements) and then your class will go abroad during spring break. Because this is a spring semester program, fees are added to your spring tuition (scholarship cadets, that means this is essentially free). Non-scholarship cadets fear not, financial aid can be applied to this program and there are scholarships available. Attached is a flyer for the program in London. You can also find the flyer at THIS LINK.

Cadet Hagood is the Assistant Program Leader for London and will be traveling with the class. Contact him if you have any questions.

Important PT Dates


We have a few important dates coming up for PT:

25 October - Groups A&B will be running outside.
27 October - Groups C&D will be running outside.
01 November - Halloween PT. Yes, it's the day AFTER Halloween!  Prepare your costumes, but keep in mind it will be more fun if you are able to run around and see easily!

The temperature will be around 44F for both days you are running outside, so make sure to bring cold weather gear!  I suggest long black compression pants and a long-sleeved white compression shirt.  Perhaps also swishies.  AFI 36-2903 contains the regulations for what is acceptable to wear on page 101.
