Current Events

Virginia Military Institute Defends Coloring as Stress Reliever


The Virginia Military Institute has been handing out coloring books and pages as a part of its "stress busters" program, which also includes petting therapy dogs and yoga. The program is being defended as a legitimate stress reliever as cadets enter the semester's exam period.

Lockheed Martin Stocks Drop After Criticism From Trump


Donald Trump tweeted earlier that "The F-35 program and cost is out of control." Trump implied that he will cut much of that spending once he is president. Lockheed Martin stocks dropped 4 percent within hours of the tweet.

Trump Calls CIA's Assessment of Russian Influence "Ridiculous"


A CIA investigation has concluded with high confidence that Russia tried to influence the presidential election in favor of the Republican Party. Trump has said that this is "just another excuse" for his victory over Hillary Clinton.
