Current Events

More Airmen, But No More Aircraft


The defense authorization bill is being compromised to add 4,000 Airmen, but no additional aircraft to the Air Force. This would bring the numbers to 321,000. They are to be maintainers in order to keep the current planes in top shape.

C-130s May Help Fight Fires


The Tennessee fires are potentially going to be fought with C-130s. It is not known yet if it is necessary, but a plan is in place. The fire has spread about 15,000 acres.

Donald Trump Nominates "Mad Dog" Mattis for SecDef


President-Elect Donald Trump has officially chosen retired General Mattis as his Secretary of Defense. Known as "Mad Dog" he is well acclaimed for his leadership during the Battle of Falluja in Iraq. Mattis will need a waiver from Congress to be confirmed.
